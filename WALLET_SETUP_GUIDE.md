# 🎯 Deployment Wallet Setup Guide

## Step 1: Create MetaMask Wallet

### Download & Install:
1. Go to: https://metamask.io/
2. Click "Download" → "Install MetaMask for Chrome"
3. Add to browser and create new wallet
4. **IMPORTANT**: Save seed phrase securely!

### Get Your Wallet Address:
1. Open MetaMask
2. Click on account name to copy address
3. Your address starts with: 0x...
4. Share this address with your boss

## Step 2: Boss Transfers Funds

### Best Options for Boss to Send:
**Option A: Send ETH directly**
- Pros: Ready to use immediately
- Cons: Higher transfer fees from exchange

**Option B: Send USDC/USDT**
- Pros: Lower transfer fees
- Cons: Need to swap for ETH

**Option C: Send BNB**
- Pros: Very low fees
- Cons: Need to bridge to Ethereum

### Recommended Amount:
- **For Testnet Only**: $5-10 (more than enough)
- **For Mainnet Later**: $50-100 (covers deployment + gas)

## Step 3: Convert to ETH (If Needed)

### If Boss Sends USDC/USDT:
1. Go to: https://app.uniswap.org/
2. Connect your MetaMask
3. Swap USDC/USDT → ETH
4. Keep some ETH for gas fees

### If Boss Sends BNB:
1. Use cross-chain bridge (more complex)
2. Or ask boss to send ETH instead (easier)

## Step 4: Add Testnet Networks

### Add Sepolia Testnet:
1. Open MetaMask
2. Click network dropdown
3. Add "Sepolia test network"
4. Switch to Sepolia for testing

### Add Other Networks (Optional):
- Polygon: For cheaper mainnet deployment
- BSC: Alternative cheap option

## Step 5: Get Free Testnet ETH

### With Your New Wallet:
1. Go to: https://www.infura.io/faucet/sepolia
2. Enter your wallet address
3. Get 0.1 Sepolia ETH (FREE)
4. Ready to deploy!

## Security Tips:
- ✅ Never share your seed phrase
- ✅ Save seed phrase offline securely
- ✅ Only share wallet address (public)
- ✅ Test with small amounts first
